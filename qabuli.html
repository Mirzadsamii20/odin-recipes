Afghani Qabuli Palaw

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Cow Meat
  • Carrot
  • Oil
  • Raisin

Steps

  1. Cut One Big Onion
  2. One Cup of Oil
  3. Boil the meat for half an hour
  4. boil the Raisin and Carrot in oil separet from meat
  5. Mix carrot and raisin with meat and boil
  6. Dehydrate the dish
  7. boil with low gas for 50 minutes.< /li>
